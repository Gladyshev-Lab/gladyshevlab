# Congratulations to Vadim Gladyshev, Csaba Kerepesi, and everyone involved!

Our newly published study in Nature Communications demonstrates that the NMR (the longest-lived rodents) ages epigenetically even in the absence of demographic aging.

[Epigenetic aging of the demographically non-aging naked mole-rat](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-022-27959-9)
