# Embryonic rejuvenation paper published!

Our new study, published in [Science Advances](https://advances.sciencemag.org/content/7/26/eabg6082), reveals that mammalian embryogenesis is characterized by an epigenetic rejuvenation period, opening up avenues for future work on rejuvenation. Congrats to Csaba and the rest of the team!
