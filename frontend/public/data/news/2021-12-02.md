# Congratulations to Anastasia Shindyapina and everyone involved on the newly published study!!

Using mice and human data, we uncover how aging can lead to lymphoma. Rapamycin and c-Myc deficiency are potential therapeutics.

[ntegrative analysis reveals aged clonal B cells, microenvironment and c-Myc activation in the origin of age-related lymphoma](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2021.02.23.432500v2)
